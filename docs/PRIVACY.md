# Privacy Policy (iDrizzle / Drizzle)

Effective date: 2026-05-31

This app is a minimal weather radar viewer.

## Data Collection

- We do **not** collect, store, or sell personal data.
- We do **not** require account creation or login.
- We do **not** use third-party analytics SDKs or ad SDKs.
- We do **not** track users across apps or websites.

## Network Requests

To display radar imagery, the app requests public radar image content from AccuWeather endpoints.

As with any internet request, network infrastructure (for example, your ISP, Apple, or AccuWeather) may process technical connection data such as IP address, user agent, and request metadata to deliver content and operate their services.

## Device Permissions

The app does not request access to sensitive device data such as Contacts, Photos, Microphone, or Location for core functionality.

## Children’s Privacy

The app is a general-audience utility and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL.
